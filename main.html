Paris, France


Paris, the City of Light, is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. Known for its art, fashion, and culture, Paris offers a unique blend of history and modern charm.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – A world-famous landmark offering stunning views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A Gothic architectural marvel with rich history.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A beautiful avenue lined with shops, cafes, and theaters.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A romantic way to enjoy Paris’s illuminated monuments at night.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A buttery, flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate treats filled with creamy ganache.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes served with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Onion Soup – A warm, rich soup topped with melted cheese and bread.
  • Baguette – The classic French bread with a crispy crust and soft interior.
