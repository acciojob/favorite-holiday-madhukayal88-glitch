Barcelona, Spain


Barcelona is a vibrant coastal city known for its stunning architecture, lively culture, and Mediterranean charm. From Gaudí’s masterpieces to golden beaches, it offers a perfect mix of art, history, and relaxation.

  1. Sagrada Família – A breathtaking cathedral designed by Gaudí.
  2. Park Güell – A colorful public park with mosaic-covered sculptures.
  3. La Rambla – A bustling street full of shops and performers.
  4. Barceloneta Beach – Enjoy the sun and sea breeze on the city’s famous beach.
  5. Gothic Quarter – Wander through narrow medieval alleys and hidden squares.
  • Paella – A flavorful dish made with rice and seafood.
  • Tapas – Small plates of Spanish delicacies perfect for sharing.
  • Crema Catalana – A creamy dessert similar to crème brûlée.
  • Jamón Ibérico – Cured ham served with bread and olive oil.
  • Churros con Chocolate – Fried dough sticks dipped in rich chocolate.
