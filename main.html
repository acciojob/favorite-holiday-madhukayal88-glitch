Bali, Indonesia


Bali is a world-renowned tropical paradise located in the Indonesian archipelago. Known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, and stunning coral reefs, it offers a unique blend of spiritual culture and breathtaking natural beauty.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The Uluwatu Temple is perched on a steep cliff 70 meters above the roaring Indian Ocean.
  2. Walk through the Tegallalang Rice Terrace to see the beautiful layers of green fields.
  3. The Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary in Ubud is home to hundreds of long-tailed macaques.
  4. Visit Mount Batur at sunrise for a stunning view of the active volcano and its lake.
  5. The Tanah Lot Temple is a famous offshore rock formation perfect for sunset photography.

Must-Try Foods

  • Enjoy some Nasi Goreng, which is the famous Indonesian fried rice served with an egg.
  • Taste the Sate Lilit, a traditional Balinese version of satay made from minced meat and coconut.
  • Try the Babi Guling, a slow-roasted suckling pig seasoned with local spices.
  • Cool down with Es Campur, a colorful shaved ice dessert with various sweet toppings.
