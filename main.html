Maldives

The Maldives, a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, is renowned for its crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and luxurious resorts. With its rich marine life, stunning coral reefs, and picturesque islands, the Maldives is a haven for snorkelers, divers, and beach lovers.

  1. Male Fish Market - A bustling market in the capital city, offering a glimpse into local life and fresh seafood, with exotic fish species on display.
  2. Sunset at Dhoomigulhi - A romantic spot to watch the sunset, with stunning views of the surrounding islands and waters.
  3. Grand Mosque - A beautiful mosque in Male, featuring intricate architecture and stunning woodcarvings.
  4. Bandro Festival - A vibrant festival celebrating the Maldives' rich culture, with traditional music and dance performances.
  5. Archaeological Site of Dhiyamigili - A historical site featuring ancient ruins and artifacts, offering insights into the Maldives' rich history.

Must-Try Foods in Maldives

  • Masal Runi - A traditional Maldivian fish curry, made with fresh fish and spices.
  • Theluli Mas Huni - A popular breakfast dish, featuring smoked tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Bondi Samusa - Crispy samosas filled with spicy fish and vegetables.
  • Kulfi - A traditional Maldivian ice cream, flavored with cardamom and saffron.
  • Garudhiya - A traditional fish soup, made with fresh fish and spices, often served with rice or roti.
