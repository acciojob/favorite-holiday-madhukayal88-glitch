Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto is the cultural capital of Japan, famous for its numerous classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines and traditional wooden houses. It is a city that beautifully preserves the history of ancient Japan while embracing the modern era.

  1. Visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine to see the thousands of torii gates.
  2. Explore the Kinkaku-ji, also known as the Golden Pavilion.
  3. Walk through the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove for a serene experience.
  4. See the historic Gion District, where you might spot a Geiko.
  5. View the city from the Kiyomizu-dera temple balcony.
  • Eat a traditional Kaiseki multi-course dinner.
  • Try the local Matcha flavored sweets and tea.
  • Enjoy some Yudofu, which is warm tofu simmered in broth.
  • Taste the Nishiki Market street food specialties.
