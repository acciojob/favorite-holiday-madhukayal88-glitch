Paris, France


Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world, famous for its art, fashion, culture, and romantic atmosphere.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most famous landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – The world’s largest art museum.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A Gothic architectural masterpiece.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A popular avenue filled with shops.
  5. Montmartre – Known for artists and scenic views.
  • Croissant – A classic French pastry.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – Colorful sandwich cookies.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable dish.
  • Baguette & cheese – A famous French combo.
