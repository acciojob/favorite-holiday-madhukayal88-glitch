Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the City of Love. It is famous for its art, fashion, history, and culture, attracting millions of visitors every year.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous for its shopping and nightlife
  5. Sacré-Cœur – Offers a panoramic city view
  • Croissants – A classic French breakfast
  • Macarons – Colorful and sweet treats
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with various fillings
  • Baguette – A traditional French bread
  • Cheese – Famous for its rich flavors
