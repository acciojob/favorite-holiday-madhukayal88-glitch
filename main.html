Barcelona, Spain


Barcelona is a vibrant coastal city known for its stunning architecture, rich history, and lively culture. From Gaudí’s masterpieces to sun-soaked beaches, it offers a perfect blend of art, relaxation, and adventure.

  1. Sagrada Família – An iconic basilica designed by Antoni Gaudí.
  2. Park Güell – A colorful public park featuring mosaic-covered sculptures.
  3. La Rambla – A bustling street filled with shops, cafes, and performers.
  4. Barceloneta Beach – A popular spot for sunbathing and swimming.
  5. Gothic Quarter – Explore narrow alleys and medieval architecture.
  • Paella – A flavorful rice dish often made with seafood and saffron.
  • Tapas – Small plates of varied Spanish delicacies.
  • Crema Catalana – A dessert similar to crème brûlée.
  • Jamón Ibérico – Cured ham known for its rich taste.
  • Churros con Chocolate – Fried dough sticks served with thick hot chocolate.
